Open Graph data is a set of meta tags nested in the tag of a HTML page. This data is used by social networks to generate the site specific social cards.

This data helps your posts gain more reach by extending the amount of context the viewer has about the link. OG data allows for the inclusion of a title, description, images, links to social accounts, and redirects to apps in both the AppStore and Google Play.
